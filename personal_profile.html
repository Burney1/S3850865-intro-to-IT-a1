What do the results of these tests mean for you?
The results of these tests I found very interesting because whilst I don’t see them as incorrect I also don't feel like they are a valid representation of my personality. Both the Myers-Biggs test and the Big Five Personality test concluded that I am an extrovert. I believe I am extroverted in situations where I am comfortable or surrounded by complete strangers, however in situations where I am surrounded by people I don't know but might see again I can come extremely introverted. The results from the Big Five Personality Test concluded that I was 76% agreeable which I do not agree with. However this might just be reserved for my friends, family and apple customer service because their reasons just don't make sense.

How do you think these results may influence your behaviour in a team?
I think my personality within the group will be quite introverted as they fit into the description of people I don’t know but might see again. The 76% agreeableness I do think is relevant to how I will behave in a group because with a group of people that I am not comfortable with I am less likely to assert my opinion if it differs from the rest of the group. The Myers-Briggs test said I am 60% observant and I think this will be present within my ability to observe group members and their ideas. By hopefully recognising other’s ideas, I can help collaboration and creativity. I think I will work well within the group if we have a clear understanding and a visual representation of what needs to be completed.

How should you take this into account when forming a team?
When forming a team I would be best suited in a group with at least one person I am friends with however I am from Sydney so I know absolutely no-one in Melbourne. I could combat this by trying to grow my comfort levels with the members of my group. That would also help me be less agreeable with people when I disagree with what they are proposing. I take in a lot by observing what individuals do within a group which can be beneficial however only if I am able to vocalise my thoughts to help manage the team. I could ensure that there is visual representation for the tasks that the group needs to complete that way everyone has equal contribution.
